What Is Casper Bot

Casper BOT is a discord BOT made using NODEJS that is OPEN SOURCE and has multiple features to bring life to your discord server, Such as ask AI, Meme Generator, Music Player, and other random commands.

What are the commands?

Ask AI | /AI Then type your question

Meme | /meme Get a random Meme

HugMe | /HugMe You earned a Hug

CoinFlip | /CF Coin Flip

RPS | /RPS Rock Paper Scissors

Command List | /Help to see all Commands

Score | /Score to see your Total Score
